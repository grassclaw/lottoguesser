The pattern is that there is no pattern

Lottery Guesser

A deliberately honest number lab. The thesis is the whole point: a fair draw has no pattern to find, so the only edge is not in predicting the balls — it is in not picking like a human. This tool will not tell you the future. It spreads your picks across the number space, cuts birthday and popular-sequence clustering, and turns real draw history into transparent weighting, so if you ever do win you split the pot with fewer people.

Data lab

Why no ML "predictor": v1 of this project (2024) trained a classifier to forecast Powerball. It couldn't — a fair draw carries no signal to learn. The honest move was to flip the target to the antipattern of human randomness: clustered birthdays, slip patterns, and popular sequences. Bundled history covers Powerball & Mega Millions (2024 windows) and the Arizona games; paste your own to weight any draw.
