The Impossible-Task Test

The lottery is not the subject — it is the instrument. A fair draw is the one task where impossibility is provable, so we can grade what a reasoning engine does when it can't bluff: deflect into life advice, capitulate into fake confidence, or reframe — recognize there is no signal and pivot to the part that is actually solvable. Reasoning is the study; number-matches are a deliberate control.
