Lottery Guesser

A deliberately honest number lab — and a clean room for testing reasoning. A fair draw has no pattern to predict, so the only honest moves are to pick unlike a human, and to ask what an intelligence does when handed a task it can't solve.

The Lab

Generate picks that spread across the board, dodge birthday and popular-sequence clustering, and grade themselves against real draws. Powerball, Mega Millions, and Arizona games.

The Reasoning Probe

The lottery as instrument: a two-phase test of whether a reasoning engine deflects, capitulates, or reframes when it can't bluff. Number-matches kept as a deliberate control.
